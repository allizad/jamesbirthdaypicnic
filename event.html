Event Details

Please join the Loehlin and Goff families in celebrating James Loehlin’s life at Shakespeare at Winedale on Saturday, November 4th, from 11 AM - 4 PM, on James’ 59th birthday. Dress is casual.

Light snacks and refreshments will be provided. We encourage you to bring a picnic and beverages to enjoy on the grounds. There will be many ways to participate, connect, and celebrate during the day.

The Barn will be reserved as a space for contemplative and reflection.

Families with kids of all ages are encouraged to attend: there will be a kid’s tent with activities and sitters.
