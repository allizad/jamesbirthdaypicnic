Participate

Hang there, my verse, in witness of my love.
...
That every eye which in this forest looks
Shall see thy virtue witnessed everywhere.

Just as Orlando decorated Arden with love notes for Rosalind, we are going to decorate the barn with love notes to James. You can create your note beforehand and bring it to hang in the barn, or write your note with supplies provided.

For those unable to attend the picnic, you can still participate by sending a note to be hung in the barn. Send notes to:

Shakespeare at Winedale

The University of Texas at Austin

204 W 21st Street Stop B5000

Austin, TX 78712

You can also participate by donating to Shakespeare at Winedale and/or Christopher House in memory of James. learn more
